Meet Pauline

My name is Pauline, currently based in Taipei, Taiwan. As a storyteller, I love how light, color and sound could be woven together to freeze a second in time or paint moving pictures that inspire others.


As a video journalist and educator, I’m fascinated by the life stories that people share, and I’m always willing to lend an ear (as well as a camera). I will laugh with you, cry with you, celebrate with you, as we capture moments together.
